What are we building?
A reproducible ETF portfolio research system.

Who is it for?
A researcher/investor who wants to compare ETF allocation strategies under explicit assumptions.

What is out of scope?
Live trading, broker execution, tax advice, personalized financial advice.

What is the first usable result?
A report comparing optimized ETF portfolios against benchmark portfolios using walk-forward backtesting.

What is the main quality bar?
No lookahead bias, reproducible runs, versioned data/configs, tested portfolio math.
